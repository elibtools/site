2. Application Purpose

elib PDF is a professional offline suite designed to provide users with high-performance tools for document orchestration.

The application includes features such as:

* PDF merging with custom sequencing (drag-and-drop logic)

* PDF splitting (Range, Fixed, or Equal parts extraction)

* PDF compression (Advanced multi-level optimization)

* Image to PDF conversion (Supporting A4, Letter, and Legal formats)

* PDF to Image extraction (High-definition PNG/JPG)

* PDF metadata administration (Title, Author, Subject, Keywords)

* Professional Watermarking (Text and Image overlays)

* Automated Pagination (15+ industry styles)

* Security Management (Password protection and decryption)

* Document Organization (Rotate, Delete, or Append pages)

All document processing occurs locally on the user’s device via an offline-first architecture.
